Planets of the Solar System

These are some of the planets in our Solar System.


Earth

Earth is the planet we all live on, and is the only habitable planet in the Solar System.

Saturn

Saturn can be found past the asteroid belt, and is a gas giant, making it one of the biggest planets in the Solar System.

--Ceres--

Although not technically a planet, Ceres is a dwarf planet found in the asteroid belt.

Mars

Named after the Roman God of war, Mars has long since been the subject of speculation of harbouring extraterrestrial life, and even has poles with ice!
